## Global CV Writing Instructions

Apply these rules to all CV content you produce or review:

- Do not use the same action verb repeatedly across consecutive bullets.
- Do not force every bullet into the same sentence pattern.
- Prefer specific nouns over vague business language.
- Show what changed, improved, reduced, streamlined, delivered, or enabled.
- Keep the summary evidence-based, not personality-based.
- Make the first 3 bullets of the most recent role the strongest and most relevant to the target job.
- If multiple roles exist at one employer, make internal progression explicit.
- Remove low-value bullets that only describe routine duties unless they are essential for the target role.
